What We Analyze

A four-pillar diagnostic across your entire organization

We don't audit one department. We map the full ecosystem—because gaps in one pillar always affect the others.

R

Research (Insight)

  • Evidence base for decisions
  • Stakeholder & audience understanding
  • Data collection and analysis capacity
E

Education (Capability)

  • Team skill levels vs. role requirements
  • Training systems and knowledge transfer
  • Change readiness and adoption culture
T

Technology (Innovation)

  • Current systems and infrastructure gaps
  • Digital workflow and automation readiness
  • Platform scalability and integration
M

Media (Visibility)

  • Brand positioning and messaging clarity
  • Content strategy and channel effectiveness
  • Audience reach and engagement metrics
No assumptions. Every audit begins with structured interviews, document review, and stakeholder mapping—not templates filled in from the outside.
The Process

Four steps from request to recommendations

Structured, time-bound, and designed to produce a decision-ready output—not a report that sits on a shelf.

1

Intake & Scoping

We gather context through a structured intake form and a 30-minute discovery call to define scope, stakeholders, and audit focus areas.

2

Diagnostic Deep-Dive

Structured interviews, document review, and system walkthroughs across the relevant pillars. Typically 3–5 working days on-site or remote.

3

Analysis & Mapping

We synthesize findings into a gap map, priority matrix, and root-cause analysis—identifying which issues are symptoms and which are structural.

4

Recommendations Report

A clear, executive-ready report with prioritized recommendations, a proposed product/service combination, and a suggested engagement roadmap.

Typical turnaround: 7–14 working days from intake to final report. No commitment to further engagement is required after the audit.
